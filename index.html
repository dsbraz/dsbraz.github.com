About

Technology executive based in São Paulo, currently CTO/CPO of the BRQ Digital Products Studio (formerly WEME, acquired by BRQ in September 2025). I lead the Studio end-to-end — from strategic diagnosis through engineering — with a proprietary operating model spanning KPIs, governance, platform engineering, pricing, and allocation for predictability without constraining innovation.

At the center of the work is Bud — a SaaS platform for strategy and performance management with context-aware AI. Bud Coach agents (Goal Setting, Calibration, Performance & Development) are in production across product, Microsoft Teams, and WhatsApp. I also structured Backpack, the Studio's internal agent platform, and proposed the BRQ AI Research Group across four lines: agentic software engineering, AI-native products, AI governance, and developer productivity.

Before BRQ, I led the X Platform business unit at Senior Sistemas (P&L, 0→57 team, BRL 17M pipeline), Nuvemshop's regional Professional Services for LATAM Enterprise, and technology at Accenture (USD 450M+ portfolio across Itaú, Cielo, Santander, Globo, Google) and ThoughtWorks. Earlier, as Director at Concrete, I helped scale the company from 40 to 700 people and from R$5M to R$150M+ in revenue — leading to its acquisition by Accenture.
